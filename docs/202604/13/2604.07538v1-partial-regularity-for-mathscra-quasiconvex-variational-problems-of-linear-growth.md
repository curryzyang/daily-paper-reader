## 摘要
我们证明了当被积函数 $f$ 以合适的意义上具有强 $\mathscr{A}$-拟凸性（strongly $\mathscr{A}$-quasiconvex）时，泛函积分
$$ \mathcal E(v)=\int_Ω f(v)\quad\text{对于 }v\in\mathcal M(Ω)\text{ 满足 } \mathscr{A}v=0 $$
的极小化函数是部分连续的。我们研究线性增长问题、常秩线性偏微分算子 $\mathscr{A}$，以及形式为 $v+\varphi$ 的变分，其中 $\varphi$ 是 $\mathscr{A}$-自由的并属于 $\mathrm{C}_{\mathrm{c}}^\infty(Ω)$。我们的分析还涵盖了“势函数情形”——即
$$ \mathcal F(u)=\int_Ω f(\mathscr{B}u)\quad\text{对于 }u\in\mathscr D'(Ω)\text{ 使得 }\mathscr{B}u\in\mathcal M(Ω), $$
其中 $\mathscr{B}$ 是另一种常秩线性偏微分算子。我们的主要结果均可推广到含 $x$ 的被积函数。

## Abstract
We prove that minimizers of variational integrals $$ \mathcal E(v)=\int_Ωf(v)\quad\text{for }v\in\mathcal M(Ω)\text{ such that } \mathscr{A} v=0, $$   are partially continuous provided that the integrands $f$ are strongly $\mathscr{A}$-quasiconvex in a suitable sense. We consider linear growth problems, linear PDE operators $\mathscr{A}$ of constant rank, and variations of the form $v+\varphi$ with $\mathscr{A}$-free $\varphi\in \mathrm{C}_{\mathrm{c}}^\infty(Ω)$. Our analysis also covers the ``potentials case'' $$ \mathcal F(u)=\int_Ωf( \mathscr{B} u)\quad\text{for } u\in\mathscr D'(Ω)\text{ such that }\mathscr B u\in \mathcal M(Ω), $$ where $\mathscr{B}$ is a different linear pde operator of constant rank. Both our main results extend to $x$-dependent integrands.
